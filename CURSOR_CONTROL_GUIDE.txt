Q: Why does War Thunder hide the cursor?
A: It's for gameplay immersion. War Thunder automatically hides
   it so you're not distracted during combat.

Q: Is SHIFT+ALT always the key?
A: Yes, this is War Thunder's default. If you've changed your
   key bindings, use whatever you set for "Show cursor" or
   "UI interaction mode".

Q: Do I need to hold it the entire time?
A: Only while clicking. You can release between clicks.

Q: Can I change this behavior?
A: Yes, in War Thunder settings, but SHIFT+ALT is standard
   and easiest to remember.

Q: Does this work in all game modes?
A: Yes! Works in all modes where you can see the map.
